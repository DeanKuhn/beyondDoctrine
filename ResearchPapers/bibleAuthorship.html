Who Really Wrote the Bible?

You may be surprised by the answer... or lack thereof.

Who really wrote the Bible? The answer is more complicated than any Sunday School teacher would like to admit.

To start off with, let’s consider who each Bible book is traditionally attributed to.

Admittedly, the second table tends to take authorship more conservatively. For example, there is a good chance that Peter truly did write 1 Peter (or at least, he did through a scribe). At the same time, there is no hard evidence to back up this claim. So, technically, the authorship is not known.
